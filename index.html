About me

I received Ph.D degree from Inner Mongolia University, China in 2020 and Bachelor degree in Taiyuan University of Technology, ShanXi, China in 2014. Currently, I am now with the Department of Electrical & Computer Engineering, the National University of Singapore for a research attachment under Prof. Haizhou Li.
[Resume]

Feel free to contact me via email.

Research interests

My current research interests include prosody rich speech synthesis. Revolving around this goal, I mainly study the following topics:

  • Machine Learning: End-to-End TTS.
  • Natural Language Processing: Prosody Structure Prediction, Grapheme-to-Phoneme Conversion (G2P).
