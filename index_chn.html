个人简介

Rui Liu received Ph.D degree from Inner Mongolia University, China in 2020 and Bachelor degree in Taiyuan University of Technology, ShanXi, China in 2014. Currently, he is a Research Fellow at HLT lab, NUS from 2020, working with Prof. Haizhou Li.
He has published more than 20 papers in top-tier NLP/ML/AI conferences and journals, including IEEE-TASLP, Neural Networks, ICASSP, COLING, INTERSPEECH, etc. Dr. Liu serves as the reviewer for many major referred journal and conference papers. His research interests broadly lie in audio, speech and natural language processing, which include expressive Text-to-Speech (TTS), expressive voice conversion, speech emotion recognition, prosody structure prediction, grapheme-to-phoneme conversion (G2P), syntax parsing et. al.
